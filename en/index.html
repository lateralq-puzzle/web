iOS Puzzle Game

LateralQ

Think Beyond Assumptions.

You're presented with a mysterious situation. Select YES/NO questions to piece together the truth. A lateral thinking puzzle game you can enjoy on your own.

How to Play

1

Choose a Puzzle

Pick a puzzle from the list and dive in.

2

Read the Scenario

A mysterious situation is presented. The truth is still hidden.

3

Select Questions to Gather Clues

Each question is answered with YES, NO, or Irrelevant. As you progress, new questions unlock to guide your reasoning.

4

Reach the Truth

Select the right questions to uncover the hidden truth and clear the puzzle.

Features

💡

Progressive Question Unlocking

Start with basic questions. As you gather answers, deeper questions emerge to lead you closer to the truth.

🎯

Intuitive Interface

Tap a question, see the answer. Simple gameplay that anyone can pick up instantly.

📋

Answer History

Review your past questions and answers to organize your reasoning and spot patterns.

📥

Additional Puzzles

Download new puzzles for free. Play downloaded puzzles offline, anytime, anywhere.
