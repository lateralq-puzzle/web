FAQ

Q. The app is not working properly

Please try the following:

  • Completely close and restart the app
  • Restart your iPhone
  • Make sure iOS is updated to the latest version

Q. I can't download additional content

Please check your internet connection. Wi-Fi or mobile data must be enabled.

Q. I want to reset my game progress

Deleting and reinstalling the app will reset all data.

Q. Do you collect personal information?

No. This app does not collect any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
